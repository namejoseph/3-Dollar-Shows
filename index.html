At $3 Shows, our mission is to forge a dynamic alliance between the arts and social issues communities, leveraging the power of artistic expression to incite meaningful dialogue, foster empathy, and drive tangible positive change. We believe that the convergence of these two influential realms has the potential to reshape perceptions, challenge societal norms, and inspire action on critical challenges. $3 Shows seeks to amplify voices that often remain unheard and spotlight the stories that demand our collective attention and support.

Our Motto is the 3 C's - Community : Collaboration : Cross Promotion
